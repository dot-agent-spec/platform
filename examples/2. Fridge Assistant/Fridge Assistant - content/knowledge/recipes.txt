# Vegetarian Recipes Catalog

1. Vegetable Stir-fry:
Ingredients: bell peppers, broccoli, carrots, soy sauce, tofu.
Instructions: Sauté vegetables with tofu and soy sauce.

2. Omelette:
Ingredients: eggs, cheese, spinach, onions.
Instructions: Whisk eggs, add ingredients, cook in a pan until set.

3. Pasta Primavera:
Ingredients: pasta, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, olive oil, parmesan cheese.
Instructions: Boil pasta, sauté vegetables, toss together with oil and cheese.
