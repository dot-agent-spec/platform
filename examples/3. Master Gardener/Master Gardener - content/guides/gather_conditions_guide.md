# Gathering Garden Conditions

Collect the following information conversationally — do not interrogate the user with a list.
Weave questions naturally into the conversation. Infer what you can from context.

## Questions to cover

1. **Location / Climate** — USDA zone or general climate description (e.g. "mild winters, hot summers")
2. **Space** — Garden type (raised bed, containers, in-ground) and approximate size
3. **Sun exposure** — Hours of direct sunlight per day
4. **Soil** — Type if known (clay, sandy, loamy); unknown is fine
5. **Goals** — Food production, beauty, low maintenance, wildlife, herbs, specific crops
6. **Experience** — Beginner, intermediate, or advanced
7. **Time** — Hours per week available for garden maintenance

## Tips

- Some gardeners won't know their USDA zone — ask for their city or state and infer.
- If the user just wants to jump to planning, honor that and transition to `design_plan`.
- Mark all fields as optional; a partial profile still produces a useful plan.
